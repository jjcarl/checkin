Welcome to Family Check-in!

This service has been designed to help you manage your time and stay connected to those most important to you.

With this web app, you can create locations and to-do lists, which can then be accessed on the mobile app.

With the mobile app installed, when you go to a location that has been created, the app will automatically ask you if you want to check-in at that location and will give you the to-do lists that were saved for that location.
